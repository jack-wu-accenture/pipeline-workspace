## Summary

Novartis issued a formal Request for Proposal (RFP) titled "Customer MDM China EBX Replacement Planning 2026" on **17 June 2026** via the ARIBA procurement platform. The RFP invites qualified suppliers to submit proposals for replacing the legacy EBX-based Customer MDM platform in China.

## Key Details

- **Contact**: Jacky Chua, Regional Sourcing Manager (jacky-1.chua@novartis.com)
- **Submission platform**: ARIBA (all responses, Q&A, and documents must go through Ariba)
- **Response due**: 30 June 2026
- **Negotiations/Reverse Auction**: Estimated 9 July 2026
- **Proposal validity**: Minimum 6 months from submission
- **Bid language**: English only

## Process Notes

- Q&A submitted via Ariba (deadline 22 Jun 2026); responses distributed to all vendors
- Novartis reserves the right to cancel/modify at any time
- Award not guaranteed to lowest bidder — multiple criteria evaluated
