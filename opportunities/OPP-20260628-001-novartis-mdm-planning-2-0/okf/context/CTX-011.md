## Summary

China Customer MDM is at a critical decision point. The current EBX-based platform presents escalating challenges that make continued operation unsustainable long-term.

## Core Issues

- **Business continuity risk**: Platform stability and support concerns
- **Compliance gaps**: Inability to meet evolving regulatory requirements
- **Scalability limitations**: Cannot scale to meet growing data volumes
- **Cost unsustainability**: Long-term cost structure not viable

## Reltio Path Status

A Reltio-based solution was previously assessed and partially validated via PoC. However, it **cannot be pursued as the primary path** because it is not supported by approved international funding. China must identify financially feasible alternatives within China.
