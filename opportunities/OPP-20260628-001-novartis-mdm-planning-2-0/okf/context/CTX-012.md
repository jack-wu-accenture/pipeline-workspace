## Summary

Novartis China has prior 2025 planning and PoC outputs that suppliers MUST reuse rather than redo from scratch. This is a mandatory constraint in the RFP — full rediscovery/redesign is explicitly excluded from scope.

## Available Prior Deliverables

- Business use cases and process definitions
- Logical data models
- Data integration mappings
- DCR (Data Change Request) process design
- Prior solution comparison and cost estimation materials

## Implication for Supplier

Proposals must demonstrate how they leverage existing outputs. The PoC validation scope must be targeted (gap-filling only), not a full reimplementation. Reuse is a key evaluation factor.
