## Summary

Novartis evaluates suppliers on 7 weighted criteria. Lowest price alone does NOT guarantee award.

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criteria | Key Dimensions |
|---|---|
| **Assurance of Supply** | Capacity, team bandwidth, financial stability, geographic coverage |
| **Quality** | Industry experience, service delivery model, references, QA processes |
| **Cost** | Price competitiveness, transparency, payment terms, performance guarantees |
| **Service** | Meeting business requirements, local language support, mobilization speed |
| **Innovation & Technology** | Novel approaches, cost-reduction ideas, tech innovations |
| **Regulatory** | Compliance with government and Novartis requirements |
| **Environmental Sustainability** | Acceptance of Novartis ES criteria (Annex A) |

## Strategic Implication

To win, the proposal must differentiate on Quality, Innovation, and Service — not just cost. Strong references and local China capability will be critical.
