## Summary

The ultimate outcome of this engagement is to support a **Go/No-go decision** on China MDM replacement, targeted for **November–December 2026**.

## Decision Basis

The Go/No-go decision requires:
- Validated functional coverage of proposed solution
- Identified gaps and mitigation approach
- Architecture feasibility confirmation
- Governance compatibility summary with Novartis global EDM/DCAM principles
- Updated ROM cost assumptions

## Implication

All deliverables in this engagement ultimately flow toward making this Nov–Dec 2026 decision. The proposal must clearly demonstrate how each workstream contributes to the decision readiness.
