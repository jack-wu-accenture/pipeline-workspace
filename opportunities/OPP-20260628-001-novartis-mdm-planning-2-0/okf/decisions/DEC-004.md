## Decision Context

The RFP requires evaluation of at least two solution options:
1. **Local vendor or hybrid solution** (e.g., domestic Chinese MDM vendor + cloud)
2. **AWS-native custom-built solution** (custom-developed on AWS China)

The selected path will be validated through targeted PoC (Workstream E) and inform the Nov–Dec 2026 Go/No-go decision.

## Decision Criteria

- Governance compatibility with global Novartis EDM/DCAM
- China regulatory compliance (data residency, localization)
- Integration compatibility with existing China landscape
- ROM / TCO cost
- Implementation complexity and timeline
- Risk profile and mitigation

## Decision Owner

Novartis China + Global EDM stakeholders

Pending — outcome of Workstream C (Solution Evaluation) will feed this decision.
