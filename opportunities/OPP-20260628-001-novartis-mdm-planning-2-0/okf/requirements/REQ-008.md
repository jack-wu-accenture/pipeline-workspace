## Requirement

The supplier MUST review and leverage all existing 2025 planning and PoC outputs, and avoid re-running full discovery or redesigning completed activities.

## Scope

- Review existing business use cases, process definitions, logical data models
- Reuse data integration mappings and DCR process design
- Build on prior solution comparison and cost estimation materials
- Focus only on incremental refinement and validation

## Deliverable

**Baseline Review and Reuse Assessment Report**

## Constraint

No full-scale re-discovery permitted. Scope must demonstrate genuine reuse, not a re-do.
