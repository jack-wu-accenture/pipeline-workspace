## Requirement

Assess the impact of the proposed solution(s) on Novartis global data governance framework, confirming compatibility with global EDM/DCAM principles.

## Scope

- Alignment with EDM and DCAM principles
- Governance operating model compatibility
- Data stewardship processes and approval workflows
- Data quality management and rule governance
- Auditability, traceability, and compliance requirements
- Interaction with enterprise governance tools and processes

## Deliverables

1. Alignment with EDM team regarding MDM requirements
2. Global Governance & EDM Impact Assessment Report

## Strategic Note

This is a key differentiator — Novartis wants a China-fit solution that doesn't break global data governance. The supplier must demonstrate mastery of both global EDM standards and China-specific constraints.
