## Requirement

Propose and evaluate at minimum two feasible solution options for China MDM replacement:
1. **Local vendor or hybrid solution**
2. **AWS-native custom-built solution**

## Per-Option Assessment Required

- Functional capability assessment
- Governance coverage assessment
- Compliance and localization fit
- Integration compatibility with current China landscape
- High-level architecture overview
- Implementation complexity and support model
- Cost estimation (ROM / TCO)
- Risk analysis and mitigation approach

## Deliverable

**Solution Comparison and Recommendation Report**

## Key Consideration

Local compliance (data residency, regulatory) and integration with existing China systems are critical scoring factors.
