## Requirement

Supplier must accept and commit to Novartis Environmental Sustainability (ES) criteria as outlined in Annex A of the RFP. ES compliance will be added to any future contract.

## Novartis ES Commitments Required of Supplier

- **Carbon neutral by 2030**: All services/products delivered to Novartis must be carbon neutral by 2030; net zero by 2040
- **Water responsibility**: Responsible water use; no negative aquatic environmental impact
- **Waste reduction**: Minimize waste; adopt eco-friendly materials where feasible

## Reporting Obligations

- Grant Novartis access for ES performance assessments on request
- Establish sustainability roadmap with agreed ES KPIs and milestones
- Maintain ES data per GRI standards
- Report product/service carbon footprint annually (per PACT framework)
- Allow Novartis to report ES data to third-party platforms (anonymized)

## Standards Alignment Required

- SBTi-approved carbon targets (sciencebasedtargets.org)
- CDP reporting (climate change + water security modules) or EcoVadis + PSCI
- Third-party environmental footprint verification (SGS, TUEV, Bureau Veritas, etc.)

## Submission Note

Must submit Annex A acceptance via Ariba. If not accepted, complete ES Questionnaire instead.
